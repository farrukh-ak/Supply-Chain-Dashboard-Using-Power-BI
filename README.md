# 📊 Supply Chain Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

## 🚀 Project Overview

This project analyzes the **DatCo Smart Supply Chain dataset** to identify key business challenges and provide data-driven recommendations.

The goal was not just to build a dashboard, but to:

* Identify operational inefficiencies
* Analyze profitability
* Simulate strategic decisions using a What-If model

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

The company is facing major supply chain issues:

* Over **50% of orders are delivered late**
* More than **$20M in sales impacted by delays**
* Profit margins are relatively low (~11%)

---

## 🔍 Key Analysis Performed

### 1. Sales Overview

* Total Sales: **$36.78M**
* Profit: **~$3.97M**
* Late deliveries account for the majority of orders
* Europe and LATAM are the top-performing markets

---

### 2. Root Cause Analysis (Delay Detective)

* Identified categories with highest late delivery rates
* Analyzed delay trends over time
* Found that certain shipping modes and products contribute heavily to delays

---

### 3. Profit Analysis

* Identified top profit-generating products and categories
* Standard Class shipping is the most profitable
* Fan Shop and Apparel are key profit-driving departments

---

### 4. Strategic What-If Simulation

Built an interactive simulator to test business strategies by adjusting:

* Shipping mix
* Discount percentage
* Inventory levels

📈 **Scenario Result:**

* Profit increased from **$1.15M → $1.51M**
* Improvement of **$356K (+31%)**

---

## 💡 Key Insights

* Late deliveries significantly impact both revenue and customer experience
* Profitability is concentrated in a few categories and products
* Operational inefficiencies (shipping + inventory) are major bottlenecks

---

## 📌 Recommendations

* Increase faster shipping options to reduce delays
* Improve inventory planning to avoid stockouts
* Apply controlled discount strategies to boost demand
* Focus on high-performing markets like LATAM and Europe
